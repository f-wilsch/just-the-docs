NonCommutativeMultiply

NonCommutativeMultiply[factor1, factor2, …]

is used for all spin chain products involving spinors and Dirac matrices.

factor1 ** factor2 ** …

is an alternative input for NonCommutativeMultiply.

Details and Options

  • NonCommutativeMultiply distributes over Plus.
  • Factors that are trivial in Dirac space are automatically extracted from NonCommutativeMultiply to appear in an ordinary Times.
  • The nontrivial objects are DiracProducts and fermion Fields.
  • Closed spin chains are considered trivial w.r.t. NonCommutativeMultiply.
