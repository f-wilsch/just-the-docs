DiracProduct

DiracProduct[gamma1,…]

is the internal representation for a product between Dirac matrices.

Details and Options

  • DiracProduct is used like a custom list, keeping track of an ordered product of Dirac matrices.
  • Neighboring instances of DiracProduct inside a NonCommutativeMultiply will automatically merge.
  • Objects Gamma5, GammaCC, GammaM, and Proj should always appear inside DiracProduct.
