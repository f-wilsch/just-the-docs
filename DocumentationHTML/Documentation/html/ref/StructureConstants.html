StructureConstants

StructureConstants[alg]

returns the structure constants of the algebra.

Details and Options

  • The structure constants are returned as a rank-3 tensor (SparseArray).
  • The bases for the adjoint representation is consistent with the tensors from StructureConstants and InvariantTensors.
  • The normalization of the structure constants is consistent with DynkinIndex/Casimir2for all representations of the algebra.
  • The algebra argument must be a valid Alg[class, n] object.
